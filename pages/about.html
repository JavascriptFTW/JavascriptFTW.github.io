About Me

Other than programming, my hobbies include gaming, reading, writing, game design, and math. I have participated in Ludum Dare, along with creating a few small games using the ProcessingJS library.

I have a passion for exploring alternate ways that human-machine interaction can be improved. I am not afraid to explore ideas dismissed as "crazy", or "too simple" some of the best inventions in history were insane, and some of the greatest innovations are incredibly simple.
